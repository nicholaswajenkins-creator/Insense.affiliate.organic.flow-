New — Organic Affiliate tile
4th campaign type tile added to the existing Campaign Type grid. Available when Conversions & Sales goal is selected. Selecting it reveals the affiliate setup fields below.
New — Commerce platform connector
Three-tile selector: Google Merchant Center (YouTube), Meta Commerce Manager (Instagram), TikTok Shop. Brand selects which platform feeds their product catalogue.
New — Commission rate + attribution window
Two fields conditionally shown. Commission rate defaults to 10%. Attribution window defaults to platform standard (30d YouTube, 7d Instagram). Both editable.
02
Payment Terms — Sales Commissions upgrade
The existing "Sales commissions" toggle is upgraded from a manual workaround into a fully automated affiliate payment system
📌
This is the most important change in the whole flow. Today the Sales commissions toggle says "Insense won't process it" — meaning brands have to manually pay creators via chat. For Organic Affiliate campaigns, this is upgraded to Insense actually processing the commission automatically, triggered by confirmed sale events from the platform APIs.

Extended — Sales commissions toggle
The existing toggle is upgraded. For Organic Affiliate campaigns the description changes from "Insense won't process it" to "Insense will automatically process commission payments." The toggle expands to show commission rate, base fee, and attribution window.
New — Commission structure fields
Three fields shown when toggle is on: commission rate (pre-populated from Campaign Info), optional base fee per creator, attribution window (days). Commission is auto-disbursed to creator wallet on confirmed sale.
New — Product selection (SKU picker)
Product catalogue pulled from the connected GMC or Commerce Manager account. Multi-select. Creators can tag any selected product in their content. Only appears for Organic Affiliate campaigns.
